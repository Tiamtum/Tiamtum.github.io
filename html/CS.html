HEX to BIN

Write the corresponding binary number for each digit of the hexadecimal number. For example:

HEX/BIN to DEC

The corresponding decimal number to a binary or hexadecimal number will be the sum of their digits multiplied by powers of 2 for binary , and 16 for hexadecimal. The exponent depends on what position the digit is in. By convention, the zeroth power is the right most.
